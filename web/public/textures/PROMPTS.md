# AI Prompts for UruFire Assets

Use these prompts to generate textures and background images if you wish to replace the abstract 3D elements with real fotorrealistic textures.

## 1. Cinematic Burnt Forest (Environment Map/HDRI)
> **Prompt:** 360-degree equirectangular panorama of a recently burnt forest in Uruguay, charred eucalyptus trees, thick ash on the ground, embers still glowing in the distance, moody sunset sky through smoke, volumetric lighting, photorealistic, 8k resolution, cinematic atmosphere.

## 2. Fire expansion texture (Scrolling)
> **Prompt:** Aerial top-down view of a forest fire perimeter, high contrast, glowing orange embers against black charred ground, macro photography, flowing liquid fire effect, abstract patterns of forest fire, 8k, photorealistic.

## 3. Uruguayan Landscape (Empty)
> **Prompt:** Wide angle shot of a typical Uruguayan countryside landscape, rolling hills (cuchillas), eucalyptus plantation in the distance, dramatic overcast sky, dawn light, photorealistic, cinematic composition.

## 4. Ash/Soot Texture (For Overlays)
> **Prompt:** Top down macro shot of fine grey volcanic ash and black soot, mixed with tiny burnt twigs, flat lighting, high detail texture, 8k.
